Sew Many Machines!

There is alot to know about sewing machines. They are used in the home as well as for industrial tasks for garments, home decor, camping equipment and sailing to name a few applications.


Although evidence of garment making has been around since 4000 B.C., sewing machines are a relatively new invention for man-kind. They have been around since the early 1800's. A gentleman by the name of Elias Howe filed the first patent for a functional sewing machine. However, he didn't do much with his new patent. It wasn't until 1850 when Isaac Singer designed and built a sewing machine that was both easy to use and functional for the well to do home sewist. Couple that with the novel idea of financing home appliances and the rest, as they say, is history. He began mass production and brought about a new era of sewing freedom for the modern homemaker. If you like to know more about the history of sewing machines visit Time.com


Isaac Singer not only brought the sewing machine into the home but he pioneered the early idea of installment plans for sewing machines. This idea took off as more and more appliances were purchased for the home.
